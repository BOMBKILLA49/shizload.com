    Why a 61 day threshold for payouts?

    We’ve set a 61-day threshold for payouts to ensure a smooth and secure process for everyone involved. This period allows us to confirm that all subscription payments are fully processed and cleared, protecting both you and our system from potential disputes or delays that can arise with financial institutions (typically within a 30–60-day window). By waiting just one extra day beyond the standard 60-day mark, we add an additional layer of certainty, so your crypto arrives without hiccups. But there’s more to it than just logistics—this delay can actually benefit you! The 61-day threshold encourages a long-term mindset, enhancing your dedication to cryptocurrency as a powerful tool for financial independence. Think of it like planting a seed: over those 61 days, you’re committing to growing your crypto savings, free from the temptation to cash out early. It’s a built-in nudge to stay the course and embrace the future of decentralized wealth. And here’s the best part—once that first 61 days is up, you’ll experience the thrill of seeing your savings land in your cold wallet address. Imagine the satisfaction of watching your hard-earned crypto stack up securely in your personal wallet, ready for you to hodl, spend, or trade as you see fit. It’s a rewarding milestone that kicks off a steady flow of payouts, turning your subscription into a tangible, exciting step toward building your crypto future. We’re confident that first payout will put a smile on your face—and keep you motivated for every one that follows!
